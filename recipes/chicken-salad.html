Chicken Salad Recipe

A bowl of delicious chicken salad

A savory flavor combined with just the right amount of zing ensures that this chicken salad recipe will be a hit at your next family picnic!

Ingredients

  • 6oz of shredded chicken
  • 4Tbps of mayo
  • 3Tbsp of dill relish

Steps

  1. Put your shredded chicken in a bowl, seperate as needed.
  2. Add 4Tbsp of mayo to bowl and stir thoroughly.
  3. Measure 3Tbsp of dill relish and drain excess juice.
  4. Add relish to bowl and stir thoroughly
  5. Season to taste.
  6. Refrigerate and enjoy!
