Scrambled Eggs with Chorizo Recipe

A skillet of fluffy eggs paired with spicy chorizo sausage

A simple, but delicious pairing of fluffy scrambled eggs and spicy chorizo sausage will leave your tastebuds screaming for more and your stomach full & satisfied.

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1Tbsp Heavy Whipping Cream
  • 1/4 cup Mexican-style chorizo
  • Cooking spray
  • Salt & Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Grease a nonstick skillet with cooking spray; warm over medium-high heat.
  2. Cook and stir chorizo until browned.
  3. Whisk together eggs, heavy whipping cream, and salt & pepper until thoroughly combined.
  4. Pour eggs over chorizo; cook and stir until eggs are set.
  5. Season to taste.
  6. Enjoy!
