Easy Pepperoni Pizza Recipe

A savory pepperoni pizza

Everybody loves pizza, and this recipe makes it about as quick and easy to make as possible!

Ingredients

  • 1 premade pizza crust
  • ~ 0.5cups of your preferred pizza sauce
  • ~ 1.5cups of your preferred shredded cheese blend
  • ~ 28 slices of your favorite pepperoni
  • (OPTIONAL)> A small amount of olive oil

Steps

  1. (OPTIONAL)> Brush pizza crush with a thin layer of olive oil.
  2. Measure out pizza sauce and spread evenly across pizza crust(be sure to leave room at the edges if you're using thin crust or flatbread!).
  3. Measure out cheese and spread evenly across pizza crust (again, mind those edges!).
  4. Place pepperoni slices evenly across your pizza surface.
  5. Bake pizza according to premade pizza crust instructions.
  6. Let sit for a few minutes and cut to preference.
  7. Enjoy!
